## Findings

### Minor / expected (not blocking)

1. **Analytics is a no-op until the Plausible account exists** (workstream A1,
   user-blocked). Events (`pageview_route`, `odds_format_change`, `waitlist_click`,
   …) fire but are dropped off-account. Expected — not a defect. No launch metrics
   until the account is created.
2. **Results-only leagues show an older "updated" date than live leagues.**
   Poland/Finland read "updated 2026-07-16" while live leagues show 07-17, because
   the daily refresh doesn't rebuild results-only leagues (there's no new data).
   This is technically honest but could read as staleness to a user. Low priority;
   a future pass could reframe as "last result: <date>". 
3. **Screenshot artifact, not a bug:** the mobile home "Models" rail *looked*
   clipped ("Title · pr") in a screenshot, but DOM inspection confirms
   `scrollWidth == clientWidth` — the full "Title · pre" text is present and the
   row is not clipped. No action.

### Watch item for launch

- The next nightly refresh (2026-07-18) will be the **first CI run** to execute
  `scripts/build_weekly_recap.py` — the H1 commit landed after today's 11:00 UTC
  run. The generator + workflow wiring were verified locally, so risk is low, but
  confirm `webapp/data/weekly.js` and `/weekly/` refresh cleanly after that run.
